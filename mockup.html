What Is a Toy Library?

More toys. Less clutter. More community.

A toy library is exactly what it sounds like — a library for toys. Members pay a small annual fee and can borrow quality educational toys, puzzles, games, and outdoor equipment for their children, returning them when their child is ready for something new.

🧸

Borrow from 500+ toys

A curated catalogue of quality toys for all ages — from babies to big kids.

🌱

Save money & the planet

No clutter, no waste. Great toys at a fraction of retail cost.

🤝

Community run, community loved

Volunteer-run and proudly not-for-profit since 1978.

The Process

Three easy steps

1
📋

Join & Browse

Sign up as a member online or at the library. Browse our toy catalogue and choose what you'd like to borrow.

2
🎁

Borrow

Visit during opening hours to collect your toys. Each toy comes clean, complete, and ready to play with.

3
🔄

Return & Repeat

Return toys when your child is done (usually within 4–6 weeks). Then borrow something new!
